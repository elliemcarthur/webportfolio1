7 Biggest Challenges of Web Design

1. Integration

There are many third-party applications out there. It is important to ensure that the design of the page will be allowed to synchronize with these applications.

2. Accessibility

Ensuring that the website is accessible to the elderly, deaf, and/or hearing impaired or anyone else who has a disability and less priviledged.

3. Responsive

This is to allow the content from a website to be able to be seen on multiple browers, screens, and platforms.

4. Security

This is to ensure that your website is protected against malware, hackers, and delusion.

5. User Experience

To ensure that the website ensures a quality experience, that is easy to use and not boring.

6. Speed

Avoid issues that would make the website take a long time to load.

7. Retention

Find ways to ensure that users/visitors will come back to your website.
